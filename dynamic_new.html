Previous Projects

NYPA SCPP - BLACK START

Design-build project - Professional engineering procurement and installation to provide black start capability for NYPA SCPP Hell Gate and Harlem River. Work include installation of (2) 1MW diesel generators with auxiliary equipment ,modification of the LM6000 TCP sequence and work at 138kV Con Edison switchyard.

NYPH SOLAR TURBINE

GMP Contract, Installation of new 7.5 MEGAWATT Solar Turbine together with RENTECH High Pressure Heat Recovery Steam Generator, Duct Burners, Heat Exchangers, integrated control system, High Pressure gas piping system, as well as High Pressure Steam and Condensate Piping systems.

ST.JOHN UNIVERSITY

Installation of new Con Edison 27kV feeder, 27 to 5kV Transformer, 27 kV and 5 kV Substation Switchgear, campus wide distribution of 5 kV feeders, including excavation and new Manholes throughout the campus. Installed Pad mounted Transformers with associated electrical equipment. Coordination with Con Edison and SJU of switching over services to various buildings. Installation of two 800 kW & 250kW Emergency Generators for life safety and essential services. Installed integrated control system,

NYPA BRONX ZOO

Installed a 200 kW fuel cell generator in the basement of the Madagascar building at the Bronx Zoo. The fuel cell operates in parallel with an existing Bronx Zoo co-generation facility as well as the Con Edison power grid to reduce the facility’s electric requirements by 200 kW while also generating heat.
